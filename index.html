FAQ
Why should I have live music at my wedding?

Through my years of experience performing at weddings and receiving feedback with couples, their families and guests, one thing has become clear... music (along with food) is one of the most talked-about parts of the day. It's what people remember, what sets the atmosphere, and what keeps the energy flowing from the ceremony to the dance floor. Music isn't just background noise. It creates the moments you'll never forget. It sets the tone for the day, brings people together, and turns a gathering into a celebration. Whether it's the emotion of walking down the aisle, the joy of a packed dance floor, or the singalongs that happen when no one wants the night to end, music is what makes it all come alive.

Why choose me?

I take care of your wedding music from start to finish in one simple booking. From live acoustic music for your ceremony and canapés to a high energy DJ set for your reception, I ensure the perfect soundtrack for every moment of your big day. I also personalise your aisle song and play all the key moments including your first dance so your wedding feels uniquely yours. With years of experience and a professional approach, I handle everything seamlessly, so you can relax and enjoy your day without a worry.

How is this different from hiring multiple vendors or using a playlist?

Hiring separate artists means juggling different contracts, sound setups, and styles. A playlist can't read the room, adapt in real-time, or create emotion. With me, you get consistency, flow, and personalisation across your entire day — no awkward gaps, no missed moments.

What kind of music do you play?

My acoustic sets are based on my song list and I always play to suit the vibe of the room — whether it's something relaxed or a bit more upbeat. As a DJ, I mix everything from crowd favourites to modern hits and know how to keep the energy flowing all night. Your guests will feel it, and they'll remember it.

Can we choose the music?

Yes, absolutely. I'll learn your aisle song of choice, and you're welcome to select songs for the signing of the register and your exit as a newly married couple from my song list. During canapés, I'll read the room and play to the vibe, and when it comes to the DJ set, you can have as much input as you like — whether that's full control or leaving it in my hands.

We don't know what songs we want yet — is that a problem?

Not at all. I'll guide you through the process and help you choose songs that match your style and moments. Whether you have a full playlist or no idea where to start, I've got you.

We're having a small wedding — is this still right for us?

Absolutely. Smaller weddings often feel even more intimate and emotional, and live music can really elevate those moments. My package is fully scalable to suit your venue and guest size. That said, if you're keeping things low-key and not planning an evening reception, it might be worth chatting to me about whether just the acoustic part of my service would be the best fit for your day.

Can one person really handle music for the entire day?

Yes — and that's exactly what makes this service so powerful. I provide a full-day experience: beautiful live acoustic music for your ceremony and canapés, then transition into a professional DJ set that fills the dance floor all night. One artist. One setup. One seamless, unforgettable soundtrack.

Do you provide all the sound equipment and lighting?

Yes, I provide all the sound equipment — and it's top quality. I also bring lighting for the DJ set to help create the right atmosphere. All I need is access to a standard power outlet. If your ceremony is outdoors with no power nearby, I've got a battery-powered sound system to cover the music. Just note: if there's no power at the ceremony site, your celebrant will need to bring their own battery-powered microphone.

What happens if our timeline changes or something runs late?

It's all good — I've played over 800 weddings, and flexibility is part of the job. I'll adapt in real-time and work closely with your planner or venue to keep things smooth, even if the schedule shifts.

How far do you travel?

I travel throughout Auckland, as well as to Northland, Waikato, and the Bay of Plenty. If your wedding is further afield, feel free to get in touch — I'm always open to seeing what we can make work.

We're on a budget — is this really worth it?

This one package replaces what most couples hire 2–3 vendors to do. It saves you time, money, and hassle — and more importantly, it elevates every moment of your day. Music sets the tone. When it's done right, it's one of the best investments you can make.

How far in advance should we book?

As soon as possible — key dates, especially in summer, book out fast. I only take on a limited number of weddings to give each couple my full focus. Enquire early to secure your date.

Fill out the enquiry form by clicking the button below. I'll get back to you within 48 hours with availability and info. We'll lock in your date with a deposit. From there, I'll guide you through the music planning process.
