FAQ

A Few Common Questions

Still have a question?
Every wedding is different, and I'd love to hear more about yours. If there's anything you'd like to ask about, please get in touch.

How far in advance should we book?

Most couples book 12 to 18 months in advance, particularly for peak summer dates. I only accept one wedding each day, so once a date is booked, it's no longer available.

Can we choose our own songs?

Absolutely. From your aisle song and signing through to your first dance and party playlist, we'll work together to choose music that reflects you as a couple. If there's a special song you'd love performed live, I'll do everything I can to make it happen.

What if our timeline changes on the day?

Weddings rarely run exactly to schedule, and that's completely normal. With hundreds of weddings behind me, I'm comfortable adapting as the day unfolds to keep everything running smoothly without adding stress to your day.

Do we need to organise any sound equipment?

No. I bring all the professional sound equipment needed for each part of your wedding, from the ceremony right through to the reception, so you don't need to coordinate multiple suppliers or hire additional equipment.

Why book one performer instead of separate musicians and a DJ?

Simplicity. One booking, one point of contact, and one person taking care of your wedding music from start to finish. It creates a seamless experience, removes the complexity of coordinating multiple suppliers, and ensures the music flows naturally throughout the day.

How do we secure our wedding date?

Simply complete the enquiry form with your wedding date, venue, and a few details about your plans. I'll personally confirm my availability and guide you through the next steps.
